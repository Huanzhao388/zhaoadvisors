# FocusOnStudy Roadmap

## Vision

FocusOnStudy is a productivity platform designed to help users eliminate distractions, focus on what matters, and achieve their goals through intentional action.

The mission is simple:

> Focus on today.
> Focus on yourself.
> Focus on progress.
> Become the best version of yourself.

---

# Version 1 (Current)

## Focus Timer
- 25-minute Pomodoro timer
- Start / Reset functionality

## Daily Goal
- User-defined study goal
- Goal persistence during session

## Website Blocking
- Redirect distracting websites
- Study protection page
