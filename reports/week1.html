What I Learned

This first week helped me understand the difference between daily return and all-time return. Even though the portfolio had a positive 1-day return, the total portfolio return was still negative. This shows how cryptocurrency prices can move quickly in the short term while the full portfolio remains affected by purchase price, trading spread, and market volatility.
